Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information: your name, email address, and hashed password.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Basic usage information: page visits and actions within the app.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To authenticate you and display your expenses.
  • To generate spending summaries and category breakdowns.
  • To diagnose bugs and improve app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to function:

  • Google Fonts — to load the DM Serif Display and DM Sans typefaces. Google may log font requests per their own privacy policy.

We do not share your personal or financial data with any third party.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to request deletion of your data, please reach out to us. We aim to respond to all privacy-related requests within 7 days.
